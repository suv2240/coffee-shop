About US

At Coffee Park, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage – it's a catalyst for connection. Our mission is to cultivate a welcoming space where neighbors can gather, connect, and enjoy exceptional coffee experiences. We are committed to sourcing ethically and sustainably, partnering with local farmers and roasting our beans in-house to ensure the freshest, most flavorful results. Our cozy and inviting atmosphere is designed for relaxation and conversation, making us the perfect place to unwind, meet friends, or simply enjoy a moment of peace. We invite you to experience the difference at Coffee Park!"

Menu

  • Americano

    a simple espresso-based coffee drink made by adding hot water to espresso

  • BREWED-COFFEE

    These coffees are recommend for manual brew methods such as Pour-over, French Press, Chemex or Aeropress

  • Cappuccino

    Cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink traditionally prepared with steamed milk, including a layer of milk foam

  • Cortado

    a Spanish coffee drink consisting of espresso cut with a roughly equal amount of warm milk to reduce the acidity

  • Espresso Shot

    a concentrated coffee beverage made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee at high pressure

  • Latte

    a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of foamed milk

  • Macchiato

    an espresso-based coffee drink that is "marked" or "stained" with a small amount of milk, typically foamed

  • Mocha

    a feature-rich JavaScript test framework running on Node.js and in the browser, making asynchronous testing simple and fun
